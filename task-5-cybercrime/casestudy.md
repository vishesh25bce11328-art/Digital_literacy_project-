Title: UPI Payment Fraud Case Study

UPI payment fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India today. It usually happens when fraudsters trick users
into sharing sensitive details or approving fake payment requests.In a typical scenario, a scammer contacts a victim
pretending to be a bank official or customer support agent. They may claim there is an issue with the victim’s account or
offer a reward or cashback. The scammer then sends a fake payment request through a UPI app and asks the user to approve it. 
Many users mistakenly believe they are receiving money, but instead they authorize a payment.

Another method involves phishing links that look like official banking websites. When users enter their UPI PIN or personal 
details, the scammer gains access to their account.Students and elderly people are often targeted because they may not be 
fully aware of such scams. The consequences include financial loss, stress, and sometimes misuse of personal data.This case
highlights the importance of digital awareness.Users should never share their UPI PIN, avoid clicking unknown links, and always
 verify requests before making any transaction.
