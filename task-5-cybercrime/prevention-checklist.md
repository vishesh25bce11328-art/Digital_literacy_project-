Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone.
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Always verify payment requests before approving them.
4. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
6. Regularly check your bank statements for suspicious activity.
7. Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store.
8. Report cybercrime immediately on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
