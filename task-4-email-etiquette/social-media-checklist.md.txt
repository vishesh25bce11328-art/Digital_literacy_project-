# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Do's:
- Use strong and secure passwords  
- Think before posting anything online  
- Respect others and communicate politely  
- Keep your profile professional  
- Verify information before sharing  

## Don'ts:
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Don’t post offensive or harmful content  
- Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Don’t spread fake news or misinformation  
- Don’t misuse social media platforms
